Bakery

Good beans.
Roasted here.

The coffee here starts with where the bean comes from. Jamaica Blue Mountain and Ethiopia Yirgacheffe are both on the regular menu, and each one tastes different because they are different. Roasted in small batches in-house, so what you get in the cup is fresh.

01
Jamaica Blue Mountain
Jamaica · Our Namesake

Rich, smooth, and naturally low in acid. It's a rare bean and a genuinely good cup. The reason the cafe is named what it is.

02
Ethiopia Yirgacheffe
Ethiopia · Single Origin

Bright, fruity, a little floral. A single-origin that tastes completely different from the Blue Mountain. On rotation on the espresso and pour-over bar.

Rotating Selections
Seasonal · Ask the Bar

The Cafe

A place you'll
want to stay in.

Blue Mountain Coffee Roasters is family owned. Not a chain, not a franchise. The Cumming spot is the second location. The original in Suwanee has been at it for years and built a following that keeps coming back.

The space is bright, hand-painted, and genuinely comfortable. Good for working alone or sitting with someone. Power outlets, free Wi-Fi, and one of the only local cafes in the area that stays open into the evening.
